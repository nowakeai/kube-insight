# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

Project participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully and professionally
- assume good intent while asking for clear technical evidence
- keep discussions focused on the project and user impact
- avoid harassment, personal attacks, and discriminatory language
- respect privacy and avoid sharing sensitive cluster data

## Unacceptable Behavior

Unacceptable behavior includes:

- harassment, threats, or abusive comments
- sexualized language or imagery
- deliberate intimidation or stalking
- publishing private information without permission
- sharing credentials, kubeconfigs, Secret payloads, or private cluster exports

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, or restrict
participation when behavior violates this code.

For private concerns, contact the project maintainers through the repository
owner's preferred private channel until a public project contact is configured.
